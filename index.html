Rooted in Tradition,
Served with Speed.

Old Soul was born from a simple belief: the soul of a slow-cooked Italian tradition deserves to be enjoyed amidst the pace of modern city life. We source our San Marzano tomatoes directly from Campania and let our sourdough ferment for 48 hours to achieve that perfect, cloud-like cornicione.

Our custom-built wood-fired oven is the beating heart of our truck. It's not just street food; it's a mobile trattoria bringing the timeless streets of Naples to your neighborhood.
